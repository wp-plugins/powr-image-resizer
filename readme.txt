    <br><br>
    Resize, reshape, and add add magic to your photos and images. In addition to resizing for faster load speed, you can add filters, animations, captions, and watermarks and the subtle borders and shadows that distinguish a professional looking photo from the rest. <br><br>Add CSS3 filters including grayscale, sepia, saturation, hue, invert, opacity, brightness, contrast, and blur. Capture your users attention with an animated entrance. Add custom captions and customize the fonts, colors, and background. Add borders and shadows for a polaroid effect, or reshape the image into a circle or oval. Protect your image by disabling right-click and adding a fully customizable watermark.

    == Installation ==
    To install POWr Image Resizer
    <br><br>
    1. Upload 'powr-image-resizer.php' to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
    <br>
    2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
    <br><br>
    To add POWr Image Resizer as a widget, go to **Appearance->Widgets**, and drag POWr Image Resizer to where you'd like it to appear.
    <br><br>
    To add Image Resizer within a post or a page, simply write the shortcode **[powr-image-resizer label='MY LABEL']** (including the brackets) where you'd like your Image Resizer to appear. Then visit your page!
    <br><br>
    Want a more detailed tutorial? Visit [How to add Image Resizer to your Wordpress site](http://www.powr.io/tutorials/how-to-add-image-resizer-plugin-to-your-wordpress-site)
